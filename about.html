About Us

We wanted to give customers the best experience with eating cupcakes. Cupcakes are nature's pride and joy. They make the world go 'round. We were founded on hopes, dreams, and happiness, and that's exactly what we want our customers to experience when eating out product.

Our Story

Cupcakes for Kookes started out as a small business in Northern New York. Over the years it has expanded and evolved into something spectacular.

With your helpful input, posts to our blog, and love and support, Cupcakes for Kookes hopes to make a national impact!
